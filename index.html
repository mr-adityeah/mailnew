Greetings, ma'am

 

It delights us greatly to enlighten you that JECRC Foundation, Jaipur, is ranked among the top colleges and universities in Rajasthan as well as one of North India's most favoured educational institutions.
The 'JECRC CLOUD SUMMIT'22 - Rajasthan Community Conference' is the first student-driven Offline Cloud Summit ever held in the country and one of the first- ever Cloud Summit in the state of Rajasthan. The JECRC Cloud Summit is a technical conference for students and experts in cloud computing, AI, and many other related fields. There will be industry’s one of the finest experts in the filed of Cloud Computing gathered here to train and discuss about the emerging cloud technology and its prospects. 

The summit's major highlights are given below:
Day 1 will be entirely devoted to Google Cloud and its supporting infrastructure.
Day 2 will highlight Azure-related technology.
Day 3 will focus on Amazon Web Services.

The highlights for day one are shown below:
Day One is exclusively dedicated to Google Cloud.

  • First session: It will explore the background and features of Google Cloud. Like what is Google Cloud, what are its offerings in the industry and how is it different from other providers.
  • Break: 30 mins
  • Second session: The following is a 2-hour hands-on programme with Qwiklabs.
  • High- tea/ Social hour: 1.5 hrs
  • Third session: Upskilling for a Google cloud world followed by a quiz.
  • Final talk of 20- 30 mins will be given by the organizers (Aditya & Kushagra) on how Google Cloud academic programs (GCCF & GCCR) had a major role in upskilling them and how can other Universities and Colleges apply for the same.

Note: Additionally, we might as well require 50 credits to allow each of the 700 workshop participants to participate in hands-on activities.

Our vision for this summit is to focus mainly on diversity and inclusion, so that we can impact a crowd beyond; which will alternately help spread the word about this emerging Cloud technology.

We would be having a diverse audience from every region, made up of a total of 40 to 50 institutions across the nation, according to the attendance. Our Campus Ambassadors speak for esteemed organisations from various regions like:
Northern/ Central Region: Poornima College Of Engineering, Manipal University, Vivekananda Global University, MNIT Jaipur, Poornima Group Of Colleges, Arya College Of Engineering & IT, Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology- Jodhpur, Lucky Institute For Professional Studies- Jodhpur, Banasthali Vidyapith- Tonk Rajasthan, Invertis University- Bareilly, RKGITM- Ghaziabad, AMITY University- Noida, JIMS- New Delhi, Maharaja Agrasen Institute Of Technology- New Delhi, Lady Shri Ram College- Delhi, Indira Gandhi National Open University- Delhi, KCC Institute Of Technology & Management- Noida, Chandigarh University, Punjab Engineering College- Chandigarh.
Southern Region: Bajaj Institute of Technology- Maharashtra, Jansons Institute of Technology- Telangana, Rishi MS Institute of Engineering and Technology for Women- Hyderabad, Rishi UBR PG College For Women- Hyderabad, Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology- Hyderabad, Women’s Christian College- Chennai.
Eastern Region: TDB College- West Bengal, IEM Kolkata, KIIT- Bhubaneshwar.

Between 750 and 1000 people make up the entire audience, which is made up of the following people:
  • 10% will constitute students from JECRC Campuses.
  • 10% will constitute of Alumni of the JECRC Foundation
  • 25% are faculty and working professionals.
  • 5% is a buffer for international attendees.
  • 50% would consist of students other than the JECRC Foundation, or at least one or two students from each of the institutions that our Campus Ambassadors represent.

We therefore request your cooperation in giving us the opportunity to have some of your working professionals speak Offline at the Summit as our guests and update us on recent technological developments in the area of cloud computing that Google Cloud is offering.
By having Google Cloud partner our event, we would be able to accomplish our vision and cause of this Summit to impact a much greater audience; nationally and internationally.

We will be anticipating your response with great interest.
